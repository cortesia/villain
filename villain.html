Harley Quinn

About Harley Quinn

Harley Quinn first appeared in Batman the Animated Series. She appeared in Joker's Favor as his sidekick/girlfriend. Before she became a criminal she was a psychiatrist in Arkum Asylum. Since her first appearance she has become a fan favorite, even getting her own series.

Harley Quinn's evolution over the years spanning from 1992 to now.

Characteristics

  1. Gymnastics
  2. Hand to Hand Combat
  3. Toxic Immunity
  4. Psychiatry

Allies
